Welcome To One Planet 🌍

Access to quality education and gender equality remains a challenge in underprivileged
communities, hindered by limited educational resources and opportunities. Additionally, volunteerism
and community engagement for SDG-related projects lack effective coordination and awareness.

Our proposed solution to this is a website that addresses these challenges. This
website will empower underprivileged communities by providing easy access to educational resources,
courses, and support. Additionally, it will connect individuals interested in volunteering with SDG-related
projects and initiatives in their local communities, facilitating volunteer recruitment and engagement.
